My Voice



Amidst all the noise
I can still hear my own voice
It scares me for I'm aware not of the source
As if it is locked up in a casket closed

It climbs up mountains and reaches the top of somewhere
Only to find itself again in the darkness of nowhere
It struggles through hurricanes and dives into the oceans of emptiness
But as it turns out every step it takes leads into the caves of nothingness

It's blind but it can see
It's fragile but it's been as courageous as it could be
Stuck amidst the storms of visciousness it looks for an escape
And towards it's mornings of calm, other calamities await

It's a voice after all,
it says something
But what amazes me is that it narrates to me, the episodes of nothing
I bother not even as I hear it shout
For me it has always been there already,
It's just a voice, trying to reach out?

-Shreya
