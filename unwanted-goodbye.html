An Unwanted Goodbye



ï»¿ Closed castles seem better than open valleys
cloudy and thundering skies now win my eye
To enter the thunderstorm I found these alleys
I hear rainbows from the past screaming fie

I try every once in a while to open a window
but the sun and the bright sky seem to eat me alive
I shut myself in and find secret doors
I am lost and tangled within the shackles of my own

I see no sky and then wish to fly
I say no words and cry to be heard
I hear a pecking on my closed castle window
and I rather run and hide

When somebody comes to my rescue
I open the door and shut them again this time
And every while I do so,
I end up finding more undiscovered corners darker so than a coal mine.

When the winters come,
I shall crave the fire place
But despite all the warmth offered,
I know I'd still choose the scary dungeons to sit by.

When snow falls outside,
there'll be frozen ice within.
It might still melt a little beside the fire place
But to go there again I shall now forever hesitate
All to be done so well only to skip an unwanted goodbye!

-Shreya
