Crescent of Sincerity



﻿ ﻿ A choice to be made by an indecisive being as I
A rhythm to be traced from a melody so abyss


I sit beside my window or I stroll around the city
I wonder how I'm struck by this beautiful awe worthy scene
Never have I seen something so beautiful and obscure and yet serene


I wonder why bad times they refer to as the dark ones,
while amidst this darkness rises the crescent of sincerity
They forget that the floody rivers flowing are calmed by the night's whistle leaving behind all their austerity


How mesmerized are we by the sterling view and how it blends us unto itself
But then again we quiver back into reality and breathe our worries and dive into the obscurity upon which we dwell


-Shreya
