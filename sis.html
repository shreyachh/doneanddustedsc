Strong I stand



Time and tide wait for none
Like depth of the oceans you'd never be done.
A ship so lost came sailing through
Carrying the callings of all that shall be held true.

Massive pathways, collosal domes,
Yet in the attic, the heartbeat homes.
Dare not the winds blow the fire
Dare not the water shuffle the flaming desire.

Strong I stand like a warrior still,
Not too firm but with a stubborn will.
Stick thy arrows in my chest
I'll keep it there for an embellished vest.

Bring me noises of the kill
I'll make it the music of the hill
Bring me thorns from your dreary cactus
I'd make flowers blooming throughout the orchards

Bear the wound, time shall heal
The pleasures and mockeries it shall reveal
Stay in the dark or under the light
Your sanguinity shall turn out to your delight.

Give your supper a soothing blow
Give the meadows an amusing glow
Let the gardens bloom through winters too
Like hopeful rays out of the blue. -Shreya
